About

A career at the edge of what’s next

I am a senior technology executive with a track record spanning foundational roles at Microsoft, executive leadership in venture-backed companies, and board and advisory positions across AI, SaaS, tablets, thin-clients, media, education, IoT, and VR/AR.

My expertise lives at the intersection of strategy, product, and commercial execution — turning breakthrough ideas into growing businesses. I have led go-to-market strategy, global business development, and strategic partnerships across enterprise, consumer, and emerging markets.

Today, there is nothing more important to me than working to shape a positive future for people — especially by transforming our education systems to meet the new realities of artificial intelligence.
